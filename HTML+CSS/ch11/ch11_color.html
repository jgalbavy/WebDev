Understanding Color

Can use a color picker, its really just a combo of Red, Blue, Green.
Saturation: refers to the amount of gray in the color.
Brightness: refers to how much black is in the color

High contrast is key for text to be visible.
Medium is best for long spans of text for readability

HSL Colors

Uses Hue, Saturation. and Lightness Values

Ex.
background-color: #C8C8C8;
background-color: hsl(0,100%, 100%, 0.5);
